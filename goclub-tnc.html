GOCLUB Loyalty Program - Terms & Conditions

 

1. What is GOClub about?

GOClub Loyalty Scheme (GOClub) is a GOIL initiative, a life-long Loyalty Program to appreciate its cherished prepaying GOCard customers for their continuous patronage of its fuel and lubricants at GOIL Stations nationwide.

2.0 Joining GOClub / Forms of Membership

2.1 GOClub Member:  Register to be a member by completing GOCard & GOClub Registration Form (new GOCard users) or complete the GOClub Registration Form (existing GOCard users) or sign up online at http://registration.goil.com.gh. You must be 18 years and above at the time of joining the club. Membership is open to both private and commercial drivers (taxi and trotro drivers interested in the GOCard) or car owners. GOIL reserves the right of admittance as a club member at our sole discretion.

2.2 GOFamily: A GOCard User registers his family by completing the GOFamily registration form and nominates himself or another GOCard User as the family head who will be the main contact point for purposes of benefits or notifications concerning the loyalty program. A GOFamily must be minimum of two (2) GOCard users who must be related by blood, affinity or marriage. A GOFamily Member could also be part of a social group and contribute all LPs to the social group.  A GOFamily Member cannot use LPs accrued for club categorisation.

2.3 GOSocial Group:  A GOCard User who must be part of the leadership of a Social Group registers the Social Group and nominates or indicates the name and contact of at least one (1) executive of the group in addition to him or herself. In the case of a non-executive member registering the social group, he or she must nominate or indicate the names and contacts of at least two (2) executives of the group. A Social Group must have a minimum of 20 members who must all be GOCard Users. A Social Group could be a school union, old school association, circle of friends, clubs, fraternities or unincorporated religious groups. An individual could be part of a Social Group if at the time of joining the GOClub, the Social Group has been informed or by expressing interest to join the Social Group if at the time joining the Social Group has not yet been registered in which case the applicant shall be responsible for alerting the leadership of the Social Group of his interest to join the Social Group when registered.

2.4 GOSocial Group: Must be a company or an organization, be it incorporated or a sole proprietorship or an incorporated partnership, who has subscribed for prepaid GOCard. This includes corporate bodies, organisations, incorporated religious organisations and NGOs.

3.0 Earning Loyalty Points (LPs)

3.1 Individual GOClub Member: Use GOCard at any GOIL station nationwide to purchase fuels (petrol or diesel) or lubricants to earn LP on every purchase. You earn 1LP on each litre of fuel or lubricant purchased. You earn LPs to move or graduate to a higher level of membership. Start as GOClub Member with a 100 LPs on registration, buy more fuel and lubricants to build points and upgrade to other category levels.

Category levels LP limits
GOStandard 250 – 499
GOMaster 500 – 1,499
GOChampion 1,500 (Minimum)

3.2 GOFamily : The nominated Head of Family earns the sum of all accumulated points by its members. Family Members who join social groups get double LPs, 50% of which is contributed to a social group and remaining 50% to the nominated Head of Family.  

The nominated Head of Family must earn a total of 2,500LPs to qualify for life insurance benefit.

3.3 GOSocial Group: Members who join social groups get double LPs, 50% of which is contributed to a social group. Group must earn a total of 2,000,000LPs within a 12-month period to qualify for a Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) project. The CSR project must be approved by GOIL to qualify for financial support.

The CSR project chosen by the Social Group must be within GOIL’s SDG 3, 4, and 6 (see website -https://goil.com.gh/about-us/csr/ for more details on Sustainable Development Goals - SDG).

3.4 GOCorporate: Members of the corporate group enjoy discount on every purchase and may opt for their LPs to be accumulated in a Social Group they belong to.

3.5 Knowing your LPs:

LPs accrued are shown on purchase receipt upon each transaction.

4.0 Benefits and Rewards:

4.1 For Individual Members benefit is dependent on your membership level so that the more fuel or lubricants you buy the more LPs you build which translates into more benefits when you move from one membership category to the other. See schedule for Benefits for details of benefits.

4.3 Members earn benefits and or rewards on periodic basis.

4.4 Benefits and rewards may be reviewed periodically.

4.5 Groups do not earn any registration benefit.

4.6 For purposes of insurance benefits, you are required to nominate a beneficiary by filling the beneficiary details. Where the nominated beneficiary is less than 18 years, you would nominate a trustee (see additional terms and conditions on insurance benefits).

5.0 LPs irredeemable for rewards:

LPs cannot be redeemed for rewards.

Additional Terms and Conditions for Insurance Benefit

Benefit covers the following:

  • Accidental death cover for GOClub members (All members for first 3 months)
  • Accidental death cover (Based on your individual categorisation level)
  • Total and permanent disability cover (Based on your individual categorisation level)
  • Hospitalisation cover (Based on your individual categorisation level)

*Only the nominated head of family shall be entitled to insurance benefits

1. Definitions:

1.1 Beneficiary  – The stated person who will receive the death benefit, typically your family member. If the stated beneficiary is a minor, benefits will be paid to the stated trustee.


1.2 Death/Disability/Hospitalisation benefit – The guaranteed amount the beneficiary will receive as benefit if the insured event occurs.

2. What is three months free accidental death cover?

Free life insurance covers for the GOClub member for the risk of death through an accident for 3 months (90 days) starting from when you become a member

3. What is total and permanent disability cover?

A condition caused by an accident that prevents you from working in any occupation for three (3) months and you become incapacitated to such extent that you are unlikely to ever be able to work in any occupation for which you are reasonably suited by training, education or experience.

4. Hospitalisation cover

When the member following an accident is admitted to a hospital for treatment beyond three (3) nights up to maximum of twenty (20) nights.

5. Start Date

Insurance cover starts when your completed application to be a GOClub member has been accepted by GOIL.

6. Waiting Period

There is no waiting period for insurance benefit.

7. Opting out / Termination

Insurance cover ceases when you opt out of or terminated from the GOClub.

8. Reinstatement

Your benefits are reinstated at any time you reactivate your GOClub membership.

9. Claiming your benefits

9.1 In the event that the member passes on
The death benefit will be paid to the named beneficiary. If the beneficiary is a minor  (less than 18 years), the benefit will be paid to the stated trustee on the policy.

The following documents are required to claim your benefits:

9.1.1 A valid national ID of claimant

9.1.2 A valid national ID of deceased

9.1.3 A police report of the accident

Plus any of these documents to proof death

9.1.4 Death certificate

9.1.4.1 Medical certificate indicating cause of death (if deceased died in a hospital)

9.1.4.2 Mortuary receipt with contact details (if deceased was taken to a mortuary)

9.1.4.3 A letter from a recognized authority or institution

9.2 Total and Permanent Disability
In the event that you become totally and permanently disabled as a result of an accident, the benefit will be paid to the member directly.

The following documents are required to claim your benefits:

9.2.1 A valid national ID.

9.2.2 Proof of disability certified by a qualified medical practitioner.

9.3 Hospitalisation
In the event that the member following an accident is hospitalised for more than three nights, hospitalisation benefit will be paid to the member directly.

The following documents are required to claim your benefits:

9.3.1 A valid national ID

9.3.1 Admission or discharge note, hospital invoice or a letter from the hospital
